Input And Output Devices And Their Functions:

Input devices:

Input devices include the following;

  • Keyboard
  • Mouse
  • Microphone

Functions:

  1. Keyboard;

    2. A computer keyboard is an input device used to enter characters and functions into the computer system by pressing buttons, or keys.It contains 101 buttons and there are mainly seven types of keyboards.

  2. Mouse;

    3. A computer mouse is an input device that is used with a computer. Moving a mouse along a flat surface can move the cursor to different items on the screen. Items can be moved or selected by pressing the mouse buttons.

  3. Microphone;

    4. A microphone is a device that captures audio by converting sound into an electrical signal.Microphones are used for listening audio or saying something.

Output Devices:

Output devices include the following;

  • Computer monitor
  • Speakers
  • Printer

Functions:

  1. Computer monitor;

    2. It is softcopy that produces output in the form of picture on the screen. It is like a Telivision screen.

  2. Speakers;

    3. Speakers also produce softcopy output in the form of sound.Speakers are used to play sound in computers.

  3. Printers;

    4. Printers are the most important output devices,which produces hardcopy output in the form of papers.
