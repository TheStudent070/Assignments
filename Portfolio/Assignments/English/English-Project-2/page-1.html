What is Eid-Ul-Adha:

Eid ul adha is the celebration of sacrifice and it marks the end of Hajj.Muslims from all over the world celebrate this eid and slaughter their animals on the eid.This eid can also be called as "The Eid Of Sacrificing".

Why We Celebrate Eid-ul-Adha:

We celebrate Eid-Ul-Adha to remember the sacrifice of Prophet Ismail(A.S) which was the order of Allah(S.A.W) that,s why we also sacrifice our animals on Eid-Ul-Adha.
