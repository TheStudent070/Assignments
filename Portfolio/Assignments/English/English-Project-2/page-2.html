Background of Eid-ul-Adha:

The backgoround of Eid-ul-Adha is the great sacrifice of Hazrat Ismail (A.S).Many years ago, Hazrat Ibrahim (A.S) saw dream in which Allah(S.A.W) asked him to sacrifice his only son (Prophet Ismail) who was just 13 years old at that time.

Reply Of Prophet Ismail(A.S) To His Father:

Prophet Ibrahim(A.S) conveyed the dream to his son. Hazrat Ismail(A.S) answered bravily "Oh my father do as you are commanded I will remain patient" Allah (S.A.W) was Pleased with both father and son's loyality,obedience,courage and respect. Allah(S.A.W)sent a sheep instead of Prophet Ismail and that's why Eid-Ul-Adha is celebrated.
