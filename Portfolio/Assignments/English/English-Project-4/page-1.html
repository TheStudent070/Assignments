Qualities Of A Good Citizen:

A good citizen possess some important qualities.

  1. A good citizen will always obey the rules and regulation of the state.

  2. A good and responsible citizen will always protect the things of the state.He will always take their care and will never harm them.

  3. Good and patriotic citizen will be always ready to sacrifice his life when the country needs.

  4. A good citizen will always try his level best to keep the country clean.

  5. He/she will always know the problems of his country and will cooperate with the government in solving the problems.
