Importance Of Becoming A Good Citizen In Islam:

Islam also emphasis a great stress on the duties of a good citizen. We can not become a good Muslim without being a a good citizen.
      Hazrat Muhammad (S.A.W) also said that:
That Muslim whose neighbour is not please with him is not a true Muslim which means we will get rewards(Sawab) from Allah for just being a good citizen.
